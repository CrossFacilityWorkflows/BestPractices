Workflow Tools¶

This page describes approaches available for running workflows within and between computing sites.

ALCF¶

Commonly-used workflows tools at ALCF include Parsl, Balsam, and Swift. Workflow technology is rapidly evolving, as project teams consider how to best run jobs that combine simulation and AI, campaigns of thousands to millions of jobs, or workloads with diverse parallelism. Projects often invent their own approach to workflows, or have integrated with other workflow toolkits; ALCF welcomes these approaches and aims to work with users to achieve simple, efficient workflow solutions for users.
